Harry Linden Johnson —
Sound Designer and Composer


BIOGRAPHY

Harry is a London based Sound Designer and Composer working in Theatre, Film and Media. Having originally trained as a drummer and orchestral percussionist Harry then went on to study Sound Design at The Royal Central School of Speech and Drama.

He has designed sound and music for a number of productions. Including ‘God of Carnage’ at Bath Theatre Royal, ‘Richard III’ and ‘Romeo and Juliet’ for the temporary Rose Theatre in York and ‘Br’er Cotton’ for Theatre 503 in London. A more detailed selection of productions can be found on his CV.

Harry has a special interest in working with devising companies creating music and sound to accompany movement and has also worked as an Associate Sound Designer in Europe and the U.S.A
